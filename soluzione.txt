A=arccos((AB^2+AC^2-BC^2)/(2*AB*AC))=arccos((3^2+5^2-4^2)/(2*3*5))=53.13010235415599
B=180-C-A=180-90-36.86989764584401=53.13010235415599

p=Perimetro/2=12/2=6.0
