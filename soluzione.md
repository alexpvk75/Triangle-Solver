Lati: $AB=3$, $BC=4$, $AC=5$
$$C=\arccos{(\frac{AC^2+BC^2-AB^2}{2\times AC\times BC})}=\arccos{(\frac{5^2+4^2-3^2}{2\times 5\times 4})}=36.86989764584401$$
$$A=\arccos{(\frac{AB^2+AC^2-BC^2}{2\times AB\times AC})}=\arccos{(\frac{3^2+5^2-4^2}{2\times 3\times 5})}=90$$
$$B=180-C-A=180-90-36.86989764584401=53.13010235415599$$
$$Perimetro: AB+BC+AC=3+4+5=12$$
$$p=\frac{Perimetro}{2}=\frac{12}{2}=6.0$$
$$Area: \sqrt{p(p-AB)(p-BC)(p-AC)}=\sqrt{6.0*(6.0-3)*(6.0-4)*(6.0-5)}=6$$
