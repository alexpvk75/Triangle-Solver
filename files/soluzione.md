## Soluzione:
$$Lati: AB = 7, BC = 8, AC = 5$$
$$Perimetro: AB + BC + AC = 20$$
$$Area: \sqrt{p(p-AB)(p-BC)(p-AC)}$$
$$p = \frac{AB+BC+AC}{2} = 10$$
$$Area: \sqrt{10(10-7.0)(10-8.0)(10-5.0)} = 17.321$$
$$ C = arccos\frac{BC^2 + AC^2 - AB^2}{2 \times BC \times AC} = arccos\frac{8.0^2 + 5.0^2 - 7.0^2}{2 \times 8.0 \times 5.0} = 60 $$
$$ A = arccos\frac{AC^2 + AB^2 - BC^2}{2 \times AC \times AB} = arccos\frac{5.0^2 + 7.0^2 - 8.0^2}{2 \times 5.0 \times 7.0} = 81.787 $$
$$ B = arccos\frac{AB^2 + BC^2 - AC^2}{2 \times AB \times BC} = arccos\frac{7.0^2 + 8.0^2 - 5.0^2}{2 \times 7.0 \times 8.0} = 38.213 $$
